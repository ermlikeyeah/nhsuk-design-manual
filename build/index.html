Standards

NHS.UK services and products are assessed against the gov.uk Digital Service Standard.

You must design services and products which meet the following points of the Digital Service Standard:

  • point 1 - Understand user needs
  • point 2 - Do ongoing user research
  • point 4 - Use agile methods
  • point 5 - Iterate and improve frequently
  • point 12 - Make sure users succeed first time
